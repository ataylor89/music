# music vocabulary

## Introduction

I made vocabulary sheets for mathematics, chess, and computer science

I thought I would also make a vocabulary sheet for music

In the table below, I'll define some important terms from music theory

## Music vocabulary

| Word | Definition |
| ---- | ---------- |
| Counterpoint | A musical technique in which two or more melodies are played simultaneously |
| Harmony | A musical technique in which two or more notes are played simultaneously |
| Melody | A sequence of notes; a sequence of notes that creates a musical tune |
| Motif | A motif is just a theme; in my opinion, the two words are synonymous and interchangeable, although they might have slightly different connotations |
| Theme | A musical idea that develops or repeats throughout a piece |
